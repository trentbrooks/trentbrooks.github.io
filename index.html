Hello. I'm Trent, an interactive designer based in Sydney, Australia. Currently residing at Lightwell.

I work on projects predominantly in new media with cultural institutions, education/government bodies, and public exhibitions through design & art festivals. I love working across different facets of a project's process, including conceptualisation, user interface design, software development/creative code, and hardware integration/electronics. Heavily influenced by an open source philosophy, I believe in progression through sharing ideas and processes.
